Biographies:

  • Wings of Fire: "An Autobiography" by A.P.J. Abdul Kalam: Kalam's own account of his life, from humble beginnings to becoming President.
  • Ignited Minds: "Unleashing the Power Within India" by A.P.J. Abdul Kalam: Focuses on his vision for India's development and empowering its youth.
  • My Journey: "Transforming Dreams into Actions" by A.P.J. Abdul Kalam: Reflects on his presidency, philosophy, and efforts to inspire others.
  • The Missile Man of India: "Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam" by Rahul Mitra: Explores Kalam's role in India's missile program and his contributions to science.
